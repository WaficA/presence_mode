AETHERMIND LAB

Internal research unit advancing sovereign computing, offline AI and cold deployment frameworks.

Research Focus

R&D

COLD DEPLOYMENT FRAMEWORKS

Designing reproducible, offline installation flows for cloud and AI infrastructure using curated repositories and deterministic build paths.

OFFLINE AUTONOMOUS AGENTS

Building agents that can understand system intent, manage infrastructure, and respond to changes without needing constant connectivity.

GPU VIRTUALIZATION & VFIO

Exploring direct assignment, mediated devices, and multiple virtualization patterns to expose GPU resources securely to different workloads.

KERNEL-LEVEL CONTROL

Working with IOMMU, VFIO, KVM and related mechanisms to monitor, isolate and configure hardware at a deeper level.

SOVEREIGN LLM PACKAGING

Preparing and tuning models to run within resource-constrained environments, offline and under full customer control.

Workshops & Training

ENGAGEMENT

OFFLINE CLOUD & VIRTUALIZATION

Practical deployment and operations of offline cloud stacks, hypervisors and virtualized resources.

HARDWARE-AWARE AI

Running and optimizing AI workloads on GPUs, with careful control of memory layout, performance and isolation.

Programs may include internal certificates of completion but do not constitute academic degrees or represent a formal educational institution.
