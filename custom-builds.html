Custom Builds

Sovereign systems,
built to your requirements.

We design and deploy infrastructure and secure software for organisations that need systems they can trust and control — from a private deployment we build and hand over, to a complete platform running under your own name.

What we do.

AetherMind builds sovereign infrastructure and secure communications. Custom Builds is where we apply that to your requirements: a private deployment, a branded platform for your customers, or a bespoke system on infrastructure you own or we operate for you. The same engineering and the same discipline behind Fenrir and our own infrastructure — pointed at your problem.

How far it goes is yours to decide.

Engagements are not one-size. They range from a single deployment we build and hand over, to a complete platform running under your own brand, for your own customers, with everything in between. You decide how deep the build goes and how much of it carries your name — and the engagement is scoped to that.

Deploy

We build and deploy a private instance to your specification, then hand it over.

Under your brand

Your brand on the front, our engineering underneath — deployed for your customers, run and maintained.

Bespoke

A custom sovereign system designed to a requirement that doesn't exist off the shelf.

How we work.

We are honest about where things stand at every step — we do not describe a design as built, and we do not mark anything done until it clears our cold-reboot bar. That discipline is the point: what we deliver is real, verified, and yours to depend on, not a demo that falls over the first time it's power-cycled.

Engagements typically run from scoping and design, through build and deployment, into ongoing maintenance for as long as you want it. The relationship doesn't have to end at delivery — keeping a sovereign system patched, secure, and running is work we can carry for you.
