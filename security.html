SOVEREIGNTY & SECURITY

Our commitment: no hidden dependencies, no forced connectivity, no data extraction.

Security Through Ownership

POLICY

"AetherMind provides sovereign infrastructure solutions. Our systems are offline by default and designed so that computation, data and configurations remain fully under the customer control."

We do not collect, store or transmit customer data from deployed systems. We do not require persistent connections to our infrastructure to keep your systems working.

NO TELEMETRY NO THIRD-PARTY CLOUD DEPENDENCE NO FORCED REMOTE ACCESS

If remote support or monitoring is requested, it is implemented explicitly, with clear consent and under customer governance. Our default posture is: local first, offline by default.
