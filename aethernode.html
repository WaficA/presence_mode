AETHERNODE

A mini data center in a box: portable, sovereign compute with GPU virtualization, local AI hosting, and full cloud services - entirely offline.

REQUEST TECHNICAL DEMO

Core Capabilities

FUNCTION

PRIVATE CLOUD STACK

Compute, networking and storage services delivered as a local, offline cloud. Users access virtual machines, networks and volumes as if they were using a traditional cloud - but everything runs on your hardware.

GPU VIRTUALIZATION & PASSTHROUGH

Designated GPUs can be passed through directly to workloads or shared between tenants. This is ideal for AI inference, simulation, scientific computing and visual workloads.

LOCAL AI MODEL HOSTING

Host language models and other AI workloads directly on the node with zero external dependencies. Models run locally and never call an external API.

MULTI-USER, MULTI-TENANT

Multiple users or teams can access their isolated resources, with controlled CPU, RAM, storage and GPU utilization enforced by the infrastructure.

COLD INSTALLATION

The entire system can be installed from a prepared flash drive and curated offline repository - making deployment reproducible and independent from internet connectivity.

ZERO VENDOR LOCK-IN

AetherNode is designed around open components and open orchestration layers, allowing long-term ownership of compute without hidden subscriptions or licenses.

Built For

SECTORS

GOVERNMENT & DEFENSE

Run critical systems and AI workloads under full sovereignty, with no dependency on foreign cloud providers.

HEALTHCARE

Host sensitive diagnostics, imaging and AI tools entirely on-premise, keeping patient data offline and protected.

RESEARCH & UNIVERSITIES

Provide students and researchers with real infrastructure - teaching them to build systems, not just use SaaS dashboards.

FINANCE & BANKING

Execute models and analytics in-house with complete control over data locality and access.

INDUSTRIAL & ENERGY

Deploy compute and AI in remote or air-gapped locations where connectivity cannot be trusted.

FIELD OPERATIONS

Bring cloud-like capability to sites where only power and local networking are available.
