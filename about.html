Who we are.

AetherMind is a technology company based in Qatar. We build and operate our own infrastructure. We develop our own software. We do not outsource the stack or rely on third-party cloud providers for what we build.

We are currently building Fenrir — a communication platform that runs entirely on infrastructure we own.

Founder

Wafic Abass

Why this exists.

Most communication today runs through platforms that were not designed with the user's interest as the primary concern. They were designed to scale, to monetise, to retain. We are building something different — not because it is a market opportunity, but because we believe it should exist. AetherMind was started to build infrastructure and software that we would actually want to use ourselves.

How we operate.

AetherMind is independently built. We are not funded by advertising, not backed by interests that conflict with the people who use what we make. We build in stages. We are honest about where we are. We do not claim to be further along than we are.

Built here. Built for here.

We are headquartered in Qatar, and we own the infrastructure Fenrir runs on outright — no hyperscaler, no silent third party standing between you and your data. Fenrir is built for people in the Gulf, the Levant, South Asia, and East Africa — communities that are connected to this part of the world and deserve communication infrastructure they can trust, operated by people who answer to them, not to an advertising business on the other side of the planet.

We are not pretending to be a global platform from day one. We are building something real, starting where we are, and growing from there.
